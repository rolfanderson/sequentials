Comics, the Postmodern, and the Magical Real

Paul Fisher Davies | University of Sussex
Posted 14 July 2017

Paul Fisher Davies has recently submitted his Ph.D. thesis Making Meanings with Comics: A Functional Approach to Graphic Narrative in the school of English at the University of Sussex, where he is also an associate lecturer. He teaches English Language and Literature at Sussex Downs College in Eastbourne, UK. As well as studying comics form, he has written a collection of graphic short stories which can be previewed at www.crosbies.co.uk.

Recommended citation:

Davies, Paul Fisher. “Comics, the Postmodern, and the Magical Real.” Sequentials, vol. 1, no. 1, 2017.
