Polysemy Express

Michael Chaney | Dartmouth College
Posted 14 July 2017

Michael Chaney has been published in Michigan Quarterly Review, Fourth Genre, Los Angeles Review, Minnesota Review, and Prairie Schooner. His latest book on comics is Reading Lessons in Seeing: Mirrors, Masks, and Mazes in the Autobiographical Graphic Novel (Mississippi, 2017). He lives in Vermont.

Recommended citation:

Chaney, Michael. “Polysemy Express.” Sequentials, vol. 1, no. 1, 2017.
